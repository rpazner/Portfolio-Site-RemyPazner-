About Me

My Story

I recently graduated from Michigan State University with a degree in Creative Advertising. There, I gained experience in video editing, graphic and web design. I left with the knowledge that good design can make a great impact. Following graduation, I enrolled in a UX/UI Design Bootcamp, where I learned even more about the impact of good design.

Aside from that, I feel strongly about having a career that makes a positive impact on the world. Of course it would be a dream to solve the world’s climate crisis, ensure equal rights for all, end world hunger, protect our wildlife, and put an end to gender wage gaps all in one, but I am, indeed, just one person

Here’s a bit more about myself to get you better acquainted.

  • I find comfort in putting Ikea furniture together.
  • I do the daily crossword
  • I taught myself how to play the Ukulele
  • I can ride a bike with my eyes closed
